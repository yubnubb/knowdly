Browser-side encryption

Your content is AES-256 encrypted before it ever leaves your device. We never see your plaintext — no one does.

Permanent Arweave storage

Once uploaded, your work is stored on Arweave forever. No server, no company, no shutdown can remove it.

⚡

Instant USDC payments

97.5% lands in your Stellar wallet the moment a reader purchases. No invoicing, no waiting, no minimums.

Set your royalty rate once. The smart contract applies it to every future transfer — automatically, forever, with no bypass.

In-browser reader

PDF and EPUB reading right in the browser. Content decrypts locally once ownership is verified on-chain.
