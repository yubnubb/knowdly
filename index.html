How it works

Three steps. Everything else is automatic.

Publishing on Knowdly is simple. Encryption, payments, royalties, and ownership are handled by infrastructure that runs automatically and that nobody can shut down.

1

Upload your content

Your file is encrypted in your browser using AES-256 before it leaves your device. The encrypted content is stored permanently on Arweave. Set your price, your royalty rate, and publish.

AES-256Arweave
2

Readers purchase ownership

Readers pay in USDC via their Stellar wallet. You receive 97.5% of the purchase price instantly. A Soroban smart contract mints an NFT to the reader's wallet — permanent, on-chain proof of ownership.

USDCStellarSoroban NFT
3

Earn on every resale, forever

When a reader resells their copy, the smart contract enforces your royalty automatically — it cannot be bypassed. You set the rate when you publish. It applies to every transfer, forever.

On-chain royaltiesAutomatic
